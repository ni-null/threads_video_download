Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Overview

Threads Video Saver ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our browser extension operates and what data (if any) is collected.

TL;DR: We don't collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All operations happen locally on your device.

Information We Collect

This extension does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No browsing history or user activity is tracked
  • No personal identifiers are collected
  • No cookies or tracking pixels are used
  • No data is sent to external servers

How the Extension Works

Threads Video Saver operates entirely on your device:

  • Identifies video and image content on Threads.com
  • Provides download buttons directly on the webpage
  • Processes downloads using your browser's built-in download functionality
  • Supports ZIP packaging using the JSZip library (all processing is local)

Data Processing

All processing of media files happens locally on your device. No data is uploaded to any server, and we have no access to:

  • Your downloaded files
  • Your Threads account or posts
  • Your browsing activity
  • Any other personal information

Third-Party Services

This extension uses only one external library:

  • JSZip - A JavaScript library for creating ZIP files locally. No data is transmitted during ZIP creation.

The extension does not use analytics, crash reporting, or any other third-party services that track users.

Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions to function:

  • activeTab & scripting - To identify media on Threads.com pages
  • storage - To save your extension settings locally
  • downloads - To initiate file downloads

These permissions are only used for the features described above and no data is shared with third parties.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is nothing to request, access, or delete. You have complete control over your downloaded files and can delete them anytime.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date above.
